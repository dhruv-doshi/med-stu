# Two-Minute Demo Script

1. Open the local app and select **Acute chest pain**.
2. Show the educational-simulation label and start the consultation.
3. Ask by microphone: “Can you describe the pain and does it move anywhere?” Verify transcript and spoken patient reply.
4. Ask about onset, sweating, smoking, and associated shortness of breath.
5. Order ECG and troponin; show their predefined results.
6. Add acute coronary syndrome as a differential and submit the final diagnosis plus initial management.
7. End the consultation and show section scores, missed items, unnecessary actions, strengths, and transcript evidence.
8. If voice is unavailable, toggle to text mode and complete the same workflow without resetting the case.
